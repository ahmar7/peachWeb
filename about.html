About Us

Peachy PC is a personal computer technician and product service whose primary goal is to ensure that every individual is satisfied with our services. This goal is meant to ensure that every individual who wants to get into the hobby of PC building is able to in an accessible manner. Peachy PC provides a multitude of services such as PC building consultation, PC marketplace and forum support. The PC building consultation is an easy way for users to get quick thoughts and suggestions on how to build PCs in a cost effective and performative quality of life. Furthermore, the PC marketplace is an effective way for purchasing new PCs
